Mama's Lasagna

Description

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna.

Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe. A big warm comforting hug within layers of pasta, tomato sauce, white sauce and melted cheese!

  • 1 tablespoon Olive Olive
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz ground beef
  • 10 oz ground pork
  • 24 oz Passata
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 3 heaped tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 beef boullion cubes, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon each dried garlic and basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • salt and pepper, to taste
